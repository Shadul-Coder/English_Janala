Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, the website is free to use. Some advanced features or courses may require payment, but you can access many lessons and exercises without cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you can explore basic lessons without an account. However, creating an account allows you to track your progress, save results, and access personalized learning plans.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by practicing with word lists, flashcards, and interactive exercises on the website. Reading lessons, playing quizzes, and reviewing new words regularly will also help improve your vocabulary.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, certificates are available for certain courses. Once you complete a structured course, you can receive a digital certificate to showcase your achievement.
